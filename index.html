Welcome to TemPsy-Suite

TemPsy-Suite is a toolset to perform offline checking and violation inspection of TemPsy properties on system execution traces. The toolset has been developed by Wei Dou, at the SVV lab of the University of Luxembourg.

TemPsy-Suite is maintained by weidou. This page was generated by GitHub Pages using the Cayman theme by Jason Long.
